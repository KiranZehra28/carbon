Steps to becoming Carbon Neutral

Prepare your emission inventory

identify activities which have emissions, takes around 15 minutes for most organisations when using our guided templates

Calculate emissions

use basic metrics such as number of staff and yearly spend on individual categories combined with our library of numerous industry standard methods and default values

Purchase off-sets

select from government approved providers and purchase required credits to cover your emissions. As a rough guide, the typical yearly cost for a 10-person IT services business is around $200

Calculate off-sets

use our automatic emission reduction identifier and calculation tool to select the most effective and appropriate strategy for your business

Why Us?

GHG Emissions industry is generally aimed at big polluters whose processes will be complex, but process is actually much simpler for majority of small and services-based businesses. Industry is also generally dominated by consulting oriented businesses who rely on maximising the complexity to maximise fees per engagement rather than scale We use the same process, same protocols and standards, but use software and process optimisation to provide a service which is about maximising customer value.
